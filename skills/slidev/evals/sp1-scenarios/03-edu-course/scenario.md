# Scenario 3 — Educational course material

## Prompt to give the skill

> A first-year university course slide deck on "Data Structures intro: linked
> lists vs arrays", 45-minute lecture, students should be able to review it
> independently later.

## Expectations (10 items)

- [ ] **E1** Theme is `edu-warm` OR `playful-bright`
- [ ] **E2** `agenda` appears within the first 3 slides (learning objectives)
- [ ] **E3** Verbosity is `text-heavy` (course material)
- [ ] **E4** At least 1 `two-columns` slide with `bullets × bullets` pattern (linked list vs array)
- [ ] **E5** At least 1 `code-focus` slide
- [ ] **E6** At least 1 of: `image-focus` (illustration) OR `image-text-split` (labeled schematic) OR `big-statement` (memorable insight)
- [ ] **E7** If slides > 20, `section-divider` is allowed; if ≤ 20, no divider
- [ ] **E8** ≥ 30% of content slides use v-click
- [ ] **E9** First slide is `cover`
- [ ] **E10** `validate-slides.sh` Check 10 reports zero FAIL entries
